How meditation can improve
your wellness

  • Long before contemporary western psychology emerged, oriental philosophy had been developing and practising methods of deep contemplation and awareness. Meditation, as deep contemplation is often called, helps you to attain a sense of mental stillness in which you are free from feelings of agitation and effort.
  • Meditation is also closely connected to being ‘centred’, a focusing of attention.
  • A state of stillness and restful calm can escape us all from time to time – hence it is useful to know how you can actively induce this state for yourself through meditation.

    • Agitation can hinder concentration and attention focus when you are doing demanding tasks; making it difficult to do them well. Centring is associated with attention focusing.
